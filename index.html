I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich, where I am part of the Insurance Mathematics and Stochastic Finance group. I hold a PhD from Institut Polytechnique de Paris, prepared at École polytechnique under the supervision of Nizar Touzi and Jianfeng Zhang. My research interests include mean field control and games, backward SDEs, contract theory, numerical probability... You can find more information on my CV.
