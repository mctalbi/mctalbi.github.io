I am currently a PhD candidate at École polytechnique, where I work on mean field optimal stopping problems under the supervision of Professor Nizar Touzi. More globally, my research interests include mean field control and games, analysis on Wasserstein space, contract theory, numerical probability... You can find more information on my CV.
