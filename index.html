I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zürich, where I am part of the Insurance Mathematics and Stochastic Finance group. I hold a PhD in Applied Mathematics from Institut Polytechnique de Paris, prepared at École polytechnique under the supervision of Nizar Touzi and Jianfeng Zhang. You can find more information on my CV.

My research interests include mean field control and games, backward SDEs, contract theory, numerical methods...

You can contact me here: mehdi.talbi@math.ethzDOTch
